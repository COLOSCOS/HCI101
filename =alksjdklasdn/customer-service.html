Delivery Information

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to most areas within a 10-mile radius of our partner restaurants. Enter your address during checkout to confirm if we deliver to your location.

Delivery Hours

Monday - Friday: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Delivery Fees

Standard delivery fee: $2.99
Free delivery on orders over $25
Express delivery (within 30 minutes): $4.99

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking link to follow your delivery in real-time. Our drivers will also contact you when they're approaching your location.

Returns & Refunds

Incorrect or Damaged Items

If you receive incorrect or damaged items, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery. We'll either redeliver the correct items or issue a full refund for those items.

Late Deliveries

If your delivery is significantly late (more than 15 minutes past the estimated time), please contact us for a partial refund or credit towards your next order.

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days and will be credited to your original payment method. For PayPal payments, refunds may take up to 7 days to appear in your account.

Quality Issues

If you're unsatisfied with the quality of your food, please contact us with details and photos if possible. We'll work with the restaurant to resolve the issue appropriately.
